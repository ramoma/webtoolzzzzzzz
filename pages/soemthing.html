Meet the Team

Hi! We’re a local gym in
Cembo, Taguig.

Established in 2017, A’s Gym began as a family-owned fitness center dedicated to promoting strength, health, and community. After temporarily closing during the pandemic, the gym reopened in 2023 with renewed passion and upgraded facilities.

Today, A’s Gym continues its mission to provide a welcoming environment where individuals of all levels can train, grow, and achieve their fitness goals — one rep at a time.
