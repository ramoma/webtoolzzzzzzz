Our History

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

A's Gym started as a dream in a small garage in 2019. Our founder, driven by a personal fitness journey that transformed her life, wanted to create a space that felt welcoming, powerful, and unapologetically bold — just like the women and men who would train there.

What began with a handful of members and a few barbells has grown into one of the neighborhood's most beloved fitness communities, known for its signature pink aesthetic, no-judgment atmosphere, and results-driven coaching.

19
2019 — The Beginning
A's Gym opens its doors with 30 founding members and a vision to build something different.
21
2021 — Expansion
We moved to a bigger space, added group classes, and launched our Personal Training program.
23
2023 — Community Milestone
Surpassed 500 members. Recognized as a top local gym for women's fitness and community culture.
NOW
Today — Still Growing
Continuously improving our facilities, programs, and community — one rep at a time.
