ABOUT A'S

Hi! We're a local gym in Cembo, Taguig.

A's gym first opened its doors during November 2016. It was founded by Angel Santos after he'd frequently go to a gym in Pateros after working, so he decided to make one at home instead. The name comes from everyone in his family, who all have a first name starting with A. Him and his wife manage the gym full time and they also provide supplements and personal trainers.
